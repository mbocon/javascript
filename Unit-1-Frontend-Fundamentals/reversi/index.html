Rules of Reversi

Reversi is a game for two players. Players take turns placing tiles on the board, one at time. When a row of one player's tiles are completely surrounded by the other player's--horiztonally, vertically, or diagonally-- the surrounded tiles are captured by the other player, and flipped to that player's color. The game ends when either the entire board is filled up, or no valid moves are possible. The goal of the game is to have more tiles of your color on the board than your opponent when the game ends.

Select Game Mode

In Classic Reversi, the game begins with four tiles in the center, and every move a player makes must capture at least one tile.

In Freeform Reversi, the game begins with the board empty, and players may place tiles in any free space.
